About Us

Summit Data Academy is a 21st century data school commited to improving the tech skills across the African continent. Data has been dubbed the "NEW OIL" and demand for Data Savvy professionals is projected to increase tremendously over the next 10 years. Therefore we've taken on the responsibility to equip individuals across Africa with the skills necessary to analyze, manipulate and derive insights from massive amounts of data. This will enable companies to make informed data-driven decisions.

By 2035 Africa will have the largest workforce on the planet, and our goal is to ensure that this workforce has the relevant skills to not only compete in the world job market but also in entrepreneurship and innovation.
