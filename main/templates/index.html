How we Operate.

Admissions

After application, our team will contact you for an interview to make sure that all requirements have been met and that learner is ready to commence the classes.

Teaching Methodology

Our curriculum is a combination of theoretical and practical learning. First we start with the theoretical aspects of a topic followed by a series of practical projects. Then learners are given a problem to work on in a team with close guidance from the instructors. Only upon successful completion of the problem can they move to the next topic of study.

Evaluation

Prior to completion of the course, each learner has to complete a solo real-world project. The instructor will keep a close eye on each learner's project to monitor progress and offer support if needed. Once the project is complete and verified by the instructor, the learner is issued with their certificate of completion.
